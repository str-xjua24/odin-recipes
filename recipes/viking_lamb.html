Viking Chop

Return to Front Page

Description

A meal fit for a viking!

Ingredients

  • 1 leg of lamb
  • Honey
  • Mustard
  • Sliced garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. First, insert sliced garlic into pockets.
  2. Cover the leg of lamb with honey and mustard.
  3. Salt and freshly ground pepper, I don't know if the Vikings used salt and pepper, but I do.
  4. Put fresh, snipped rosemary all over the lamb.
  5. Wrap the entire lamb well in foil and put into oven or grill.
