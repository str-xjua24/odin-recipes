Springtime Fritters

Return to Front Page

Description

Even the Vikings had a sweet tooth every now and then

Ingredients

  • 6oz flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pint or so of beer (flat lager from last night will do)/li>
  • A handfull of edible young leaves and flowers
  • Honey

Recipe Instructions

  1. Put the flour and salt in a large bowl
  2. Make a well in the centre and break the egg into it
  3. Pour a little beer from the middle and mix flour from the centre
  4. Mix thoroughly until you have a batter the thickness of cream
  5. Cover the bowl with a cloth and leave to stand for about 30 minutes
  6. Mix in your greenery
  7. Drop spoonfuls of flowery batter into the hot oil, and fry until golden, turning once
  8. Lift them out onto a plate, and drizzle with honey to serve
